Severance ℹ️
Severance is a show on Apple TV+ with 2 total seasons comprising 19 episodes. It premiered on Feb 18, 2022 and has been renewed for an upcoming third season.
It is a science fiction psychological thriller about employees at Lumon Industries that have undergone a procedure to seperate their memories of the outside world from their work, and vice versa.
Main characters: Mark, Dylan, Helly, Seth, Irving, Ms. Casey, and more.
Created by Dan Erickson, directed by Ben Stiller, Aoife McArdle, and more.
